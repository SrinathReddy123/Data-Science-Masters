Z test with examples:
====================
Here we learn different statistaical analysis and doing hypothesis testing:
 -> This will be important when we take a problem statement and perform different types of 
  hypothesis testing.
  
 Types of Hypothesis testing:
==================
1. z-test
2. t-test
3. chi-square test 
4. anova test

Bith z-test and t-test are specifically related to data dealing with average like sample average and population average.

chi-square test is used for doing statistical analysis on categorical data.
anova test is for variance of the data.

Here we try to estimate population parameter(unknown), with sample data can we come up with 
 conclusion regarding population data.
